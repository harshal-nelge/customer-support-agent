======================================================
         SHOPSMART E-COMMERCE — REFUND POLICY
              Version 2.1 | January 2025
======================================================

1. GENERAL RETURN POLICY
------------------------
1.1  Customers may request a refund within 30 calendar days
     from the date of delivery.
1.2  All refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
1.3  Refund processing takes 5–10 business days after approval.
1.4  Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item arrived
     defective or the wrong item was shipped.

2. FINAL SALE ITEMS
-------------------
2.1  Items marked as "Final Sale" CANNOT be returned or refunded
     under any circumstances.
2.2  This includes clearance items, limited-edition products,
     and items explicitly marked final sale at checkout.

3. CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RULES
---------------------------
3.1  ELECTRONICS
     - Return window: 15 days from delivery (not 30).
     - Item must be unopened and in original sealed packaging.
     - Opened electronics are non-returnable.
     - A 15% restocking fee applies to all electronics returns.
     - Manufacturer warranty claims must be handled directly
       with the manufacturer.

3.2  CLOTHING & APPAREL
     - Return window: 30 days from delivery.
     - Tags must still be attached; item must be unworn/unwashed.
     - Undergarments and swimwear are always final sale.
     - No restocking fee.

3.3  HOME & KITCHEN
     - Return window: 30 days from delivery.
     - Item must be unused and in original packaging.
     - Assembled furniture cannot be returned.
     - A 10% restocking fee applies.

3.4  BOOKS & MEDIA
     - Return window: 30 days from delivery.
     - Item must be in original condition.
     - Digital codes (if included) must be unredeemed.

3.5  PERISHABLE GOODS
     - Perishable items are NON-REFUNDABLE.
     - Exception: items that arrived spoiled or visibly damaged
       on delivery may be refunded with photo evidence.

3.6  SPORTS & OUTDOORS
     - Return window: 30 days from delivery.
     - Item must be unused.
     - Safety equipment (helmets, pads) that has been worn
       is non-returnable for hygiene and liability reasons.

4. ESCALATION RULES
--------------------
4.1  Any refund request where the refund amount exceeds $500
     MUST be escalated to a human supervisor for manual review.
     The AI agent is NOT authorized to approve refunds over $500.
4.2  Escalated cases are typically resolved within 24–48 hours.

5. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
------------------------------
5.1  Items that arrived damaged or defective are eligible for a
     full refund REGARDLESS of the return window.
5.2  Photo evidence of the damage is required.
5.3  Shipping costs are also refunded for defective items.
5.4  This does NOT override final-sale status — final sale items
     sold "as-is" with a known/acknowledged defect are still
     non-refundable.

6. WRONG ITEM SHIPPED
----------------------
6.1  If the customer received the wrong item, they are entitled
     to a full refund plus return shipping reimbursement.
6.2  This overrides the normal return window.

7. FRAUD PREVENTION & LIMITS
-----------------------------
7.1  Each customer is limited to a maximum of 3 refunds within
     any rolling 90-day period.
7.2  Exceeding this limit requires human review regardless of
     the refund amount.
7.3  A refund request where the amount exceeds the original
     order total will be automatically denied.

8. MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS
-----------------------
8.1  STANDARD members: no additional return benefits.
8.2  PREMIUM members: return window extended by 15 extra days
     on top of the category-specific window.
8.3  VIP members: return window extended by 30 extra days
     on top of the category-specific window, plus priority
     processing.

======================================================
